< lang="en"> JavaScript theory QUE.1-What are the differences between var, let and const? ANS:- VAR:- 1.The scope of a var variable is functional scope. 2.It can be declared without initialization. 3.It can be accessed without initialization as its default value is “undefined”. LET:-1.The scope of a let variable is block scope. 2.It can be declared without initialization. 3.It cannot be accessed without initialization otherwise it will give ‘referenceError’. CONST:-1.The scope of a const variable is block scope. 2.It cannot be declared without initialization. 3.It cannot be accessed without initialization, as it cannot be declared without initialization.
